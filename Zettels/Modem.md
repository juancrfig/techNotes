The foundations of early physical infrastructure for worldwide communication, like the [[telephone]], were suited to handle [[Analog Information|analog signals]]; while [[Computer|computers']] design is based on [[Digital Information|digital data]].

A *modem* is a device that transforms *digital signals* coming from a *computer* in *analog* ones, making them ready to start their travel outside the *local network*.
