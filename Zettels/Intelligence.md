It's a set of traits that a [[system]] can possess, which are related with *problem-solving* capabilities:

- **Learning:** It's the ability to change behavior in response to [[information]].
- **Generalization:** This is basically *pattern recognition*. It's about solving a new problem by using previous knowledge.
- **Uncertainty-tolerance:** It's about how a *system* is able to ***navigate*** towards the solution of a problem, even when there's a lack of *information*.
